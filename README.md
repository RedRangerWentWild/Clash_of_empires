## Overview

Clash of Empires is a turn-based strategy concept focusing on:

- Long-term empire planning  
- Diplomacy, alliances, and betrayal  
- Territory expansion  
- Resource and population management  
- War, defense, and leadership decisions  

Victory may come through conquest, influence, or survival.

---

## Lore

> "Empires do not fall. They are forgotten."

Civilizations rise from scattered tribes, forging systems of power and culture. The world holds ancient ruins, disputed borders, and the fading memory of fallen kingdoms. Every empire seeks glory, but history remembers only those who endure.

## Mechanics

- Turn-based actions  
- Fog of war exploration  
- Optional treaties and pacts  
- Technology tree advancement  
- Reputation-based diplomacy  

---

## Progression

Players start with a single settlement and gradually:

1. Gather and manage resources  
2. Expand territory  
3. Form alliances or rivalries  
4. Develop culture and technology  
5. Engage in small skirmishes or full-scale wars  
6. Pursue one of multiple victory conditions
