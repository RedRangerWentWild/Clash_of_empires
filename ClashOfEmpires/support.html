Support & FAQ

General Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by visiting the Login page and clicking on the "Sign Up" tab. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your registration.

What are the system requirements for Clash of Empires?

Clash of Empires requires a modern web browser and a stable internet connection. Specific hardware requirements will be announced closer to release.

Gameplay Questions

How do I choose an empire?

Upon starting the game, you will be presented with a choice of empires. Each empire has unique bonuses and lore. Choose wisely!
